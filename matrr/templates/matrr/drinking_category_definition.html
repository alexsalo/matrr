Drinking Category Definitions


VHD Very Heavy Drinker These animals are defined by having an average daily ethanol intake exceeding 3 g/kg
AND
have >= 10% of open access days with an ethanol intake exceeding 4 g/kg
HD Heavy Drinker These animals are defined by having an ethanol consumption of >= 3 g/kg for >= 20% of days
BD Binge Drinker These animals are defined by having an ethanol consumption of >= 2 g/kg for >= 55% of days
AND
a recorded BEC >= 80 mg/dl at least once per year
LD Low Drinker These animals are defined as not BD, not HD, and not VHD.

Some animals can not be classified becuase of insufficient drinking or BEC data. These animals are: 10033, 10043, 10050, 10053, 10099, 10102, 10104, 10107, 10108, 10109, 10110, 10111, 10112, 10113, 10114, 10125, 10126, 10128, 10128, 10129, 10135
