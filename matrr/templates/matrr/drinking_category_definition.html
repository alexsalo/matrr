Drinking Category Definitions


VHD Very Heavy Drinker defined by having >= 10% of open access days' ethanol intake exceeding 4 g/kg
AND
an average daily ethanol intake exceeding 3 g/kg
HD Heavy Drinker defined by having >= 20% of open access days' ethanol intake exceeding 3 g/kg
BD Binge Drinker defined by having >= 55% of open access days' ethanol intake exceeding 2 g/kg
LD Low Drinker defined as not BD, not HD, and not VHD.
