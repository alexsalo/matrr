Please, read the following carefully!
A rejected order cannot be modified, but we can create a copy for you to edit.
The copy:
  • will have status "revised"
  • you can edit it
  • you cannot change the cohort!
  • you can edit requested tissues (monkeys, amount, units, etc.)
  • you can delete requested tissues
  • you cannot add new tissues! (Instead, submit a new request).
  • If your order was partially accepted, you cannot select monkeys that were accepted!
After your editing will be done, you have to resubmit the order! A revised order is just your draft. Without submission MATRR team cannon see the order and cannot accept/reject it.
