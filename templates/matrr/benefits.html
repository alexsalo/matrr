Account Benefits

Signing up for an account at MATRR is not mandatory, although it has numerous benefits.

  • With an account you will be able to request tissue from this resource;
  • You will be able to conduct advanced searches or our repository to identify tissues of particular interest to your research;
  • You will be kept up-to-date about changes made to MATRR and collaborative research opportunities
